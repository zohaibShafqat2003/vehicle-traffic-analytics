- **Detection**: locating objects with bounding boxes.
- **Tracking**: linking detections across frames with a unique track_id.
- **Track ID**: identifier for the same physical vehicle across frames.
- **ROI**: region of interest (polygon area in the frame).
- **Line crossing**: counting rule based on crossing a virtual line.
- **Bucket**: a time interval group (15 minutes here).
- **mAP**: mean average precision (detection metric).
- **ID switch**: when the tracker changes identity of an object.
- **MAPE**: mean absolute percentage error (counting accuracy metric).
